Oatmeal Yum Yum

Yummy oat meal with a special twist

This recipe is delicious and great fun for the whole family.

Kids, please do not try this at home.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Liquid (milk works great!)
  • Peanut butter
  • Walnuts (chopped please)
  • Berries (blue or other)

Steps

  1. You're gonna need a bowl
  2. 1/2 cup of oats go into that bowl
  3. Add 1 cup liquid
  4. Microwave for 2 minutes and keep an eye out for overflow
  5. Remove from microwave and stir
  6. Microwave for 1 more minute
  7. Remove and stir in a heaping spoonful of peanut butter
  8. Sprinkle walnuts and berries on top
  9. Eat it up!

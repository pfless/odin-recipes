Lasagna

Yummy lasagna with those flat noodles, cheese, meat and sauce

Picture this: yummy in my tummy dinner of lasagna. It's like a pasta cake. Seriously, you can't go wrong. Talk about YUM!!!

Not for the faint of heart. Don't tr y this recipe at home kids.

Ingredients

  • Those flat noodles
  • That red sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese I think
  • Some spices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to like 425
  2. In a pan, lay down one layer of those flat noodles
  3. Layer of that red sauce on noodle layer
  4. Layer of cheese on sauce layer
  5. Layer of ground beef on cheese layer
  6. Throw some spices on that
  7. Repeat steps 2-6 until pan is full
  8. One more layer of cheese on top of that bad boy
  9. Slap the pan in the oven for like one hour
  10. When cheese is melty on top, remove from oven and let cool because you don't want to burn your tongue
  11. Enjoy your lasagna!
